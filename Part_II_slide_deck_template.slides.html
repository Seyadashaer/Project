Investigation Overview¶

The purpose of this project is to analyze a dataset of cats, focusing on key features such as age, weight, breed, and gender. The goal is to uncover trends, distributions, and relationships that are meaningful for understanding characteristics across different cat breeds.

Key insights include:

  • Age Distribution: Most cats fall within the range of 1 to 16 years, with some breeds having longer lifespans.
  • Weight Trends: Male cats tend to weigh more than females, with breed playing a significant role in average weight.
  • Multivariate Insights: Certain breeds exhibit distinct patterns in age and weight distributions when segmented by gender.

Dataset Overview¶

The dataset contains information about cats, including variables like age (in years), weight (in kg), breed, and gender. It provides a comprehensive overview of these features across multiple breeds, allowing for univariate, bivariate, and multivariate analyses. The data is clean and suitable for immediate analysis with minimal preprocessing required.
